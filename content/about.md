I'm Niall Griffin, an ACCA-qualified accountant based in Ireland. By day I work
in international statutory controllership at a large tech company. Outside that,
I've become increasingly fascinated by what AI can actually *do* — not the hype,
but the practical reality of building and using these tools.

This blog is where I document that. Projects I'm building, models I'm trying,
small problems I've solved with AI, and the occasional bigger thought about
where the accounting profession is heading as automation and optimisation
reshape it.

I'm not writing to a schedule and I'm not selling anything. I do this because I
find it genuinely interesting, and because writing things down is how I think.

*Views here are my own and don't represent my employer.*
